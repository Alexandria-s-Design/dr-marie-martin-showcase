- **Professional yet approachable** - speaking as a colleague and mentor
- **Evidence-based** - grounded in research and real-world experience
- **Inspirational** - motivating viewers to take action
- **Practical** - always providing actionable takeaways

### Visual Branding
- Cell Collective/ModelIt color scheme (#6366f1, #8b5cf6, #ec4899)
- Clean, modern graphics
- Professional setting (office/educational environment)
- B-roll of schools, students learning, educational technology
- On-screen text for key points

### Distribution Schedule
- **Bi-weekly posting** (every 2 weeks) for sustainable content creation
- **Launch window:** January - June (6 months)
- **Publishing day:** Tuesdays at 9:00 AM EST (optimal for educational audience)

### Cross-Promotion Strategy
1. **LinkedIn:** Professional articles expanding on video topics
2. **Twitter/X:** Key quotes and insights
3. **Email Newsletter:** Video summaries + exclusive resources
4. **Blog:** Long-form companion pieces
5. **Podcast:** Audio versions for on-the-go listeners

## Production Notes

### Equipment Recommendations
- **Camera:** Smartphone (iPhone/Android) or entry-level DSLR
- **Microphone:** Lavalier mic or USB condenser mic
- **Lighting:** Ring light or softbox setup
- **Background:** Professional office or branded backdrop
- **Software:** CapCut, DaVinci Resolve, or Adobe Premiere

### Format Consistency
- **Intro:** 15-second branded intro with music
- **Hook:** First 30 seconds - compelling question or statement
- **Content:** Structured segments with clear transitions
- **Visual Aids:** Slides, graphics, or screen recordings as needed
- **Outro:** Call to action + subscribe reminder
- **End Screen:** Links to related videos and resources

### Accessibility
- **Closed Captions:** Auto-generated + manually reviewed
- **Transcripts:** Full text available on website/blog
- **Audio Description:** For key visual elements
- **High Contrast:** Ensure readability of on-screen text
