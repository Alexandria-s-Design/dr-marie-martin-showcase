About Dr. Marie Martin

Dr. Marie Martin is a pioneering educator and thought leader in computational modeling and systems biology education. As a key collaborator with Cell Collective and ModelIt, she has been instrumental in bringing cutting-edge computational modeling tools into K-12 and higher education classrooms worldwide.

Through her work at Discovery Collective and in partnership with the University of Nebraska, Dr. Martin has helped design and pilot innovative educational platforms that make complex biological systems accessible to students without requiring coding or advanced mathematical knowledge. Her approach emphasizes hands-on, interactive modeling that transforms how students understand and engage with science.

Dr. Martin is a passionate advocate for the intelligent age of education, believing that computational modeling and systems thinking are essential skills for the next generation. She works to redefine what "modeling" means in schools—moving beyond paper and cardboard to dynamic, predictive simulations that mirror real-world scientific research.

Her contributions to educational technology include developing student-centered curricula, creating ambassador programs that amplify student voices in edtech design, and championing pedagogical transformation through innovative tools that bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application.
