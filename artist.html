The Voice Behind the Music

Dr. Marie Martin, EdD is an Educational Leadership Consultant with 25+ years transforming schools, districts, and government education programs across K-12, higher education, and military sectors.

A former district administrator and government education specialist, she co-founded Alexandria's Design to help educational organizations embrace future-ready practices. Her expertise spans strategic planning, curriculum development, equity-focused transformation, and AI integration in education.

As a jazz vocalist, Marie brings the same passion for transformation to her music—blending heritage, storytelling, and the improvisational spirit of New Orleans jazz into performances that educate and inspire.

"I've seen every education trend come and go. This isn't a trend—this is the future. Let me show you how to lead it."
